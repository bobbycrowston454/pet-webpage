These Are Our Favorite Places

These are just two places we like to visit when we want to get away from the business of life.

Cabo, Mexico

The Cabo beaches are a very warm and welcoming. The water is warm and very clear making it fun to swim and snorkle in.

Stuff i like to do in cabo

  • Swimming
  • Surfing
  • Bartering

Mendocino, CA

Mendocino California is a rugged and isolated place. I love the cliffs and the tempestuous waves. The smell of the sea in the air is refeshing and enlivening. I very much enjoyed my stay there. Although, the one night we spent in the hotel there was a very drunk woman in the lobby talking about England and she was very annoying.

Stuff i like to do in mendocino

  • Hike
  • Relax
  • Swim

If you ever get the chance to visit one of these places, we suggest you take it!
